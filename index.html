Empowering your future with practical skills. We offer a wide range of free and paid courses, both online and offline, to help you build your career. Join us and learn from industry experts!

Introduction

PMF Skill Institute is dedicated to providing high-quality, industry-relevant training to empower individuals for the modern workforce. Our NSDA-affiliated institute offers a blend of technical, creative, and vocational courses, ensuring every learner can find a path to success. With a focus on hands-on learning, research, and community development, we strive to build a skilled, confident, and future-ready generation.

PMF Skills Institute - Research Sectors

  • Public Health and Epidemiology
    Research on disease patterns, healthcare systems, and community health improvement strategies.
  • Environmental Sustainability & Climate Change
    Focused on environmental protection, sustainable practices, and climate resilience in vulnerable communities.
  • TVET and Skills Development
    Studies on technical and vocational education, labor market needs, and youth employability.
  • Women's Empowerment and Gender Studies
    Research on gender equality, women's rights, and inclusive development policies.
  • Child Rights and Protection
    Investigations into child welfare, education access, protection policies, and institutional support systems.
  • Migration and Labor Market
    Analysis of internal and external migration, labor dynamics, and socioeconomic impacts.
  • Agriculture and Food Security
    Research on sustainable agriculture, rural livelihoods, and resilience in food systems.
  • Digital Inclusion and ICT
    Studies on access to digital tools, ICT training, and reducing the digital divide.
  • Urban Development and WASH
    Research on urban planning, informal settlements, and access to safe water, sanitation, and hygiene.
  • Project Proposal for Construction (Level-6)
    Advanced research and proposal writing for construction projects and infrastructure development.

We encourage students to participate in research and project-based learning. Our institute provides resources and guidance for academic and practical research, innovation, and skill-based projects. Contact us to know more about ongoing research opportunities and how you can get involved!
